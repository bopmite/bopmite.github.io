    Consistently inconsistent

    In our design approach, we adhere to a central principle of "consistently inconsistent." While the overall width and layout of our website remain constant, creating a structured and user-friendly experience, we purposely diverge in our use of fonts, button designs, and box designs. This intentional inconsistency allows for creativity, playfulness, and an engaging visual hierarchy that captures users' attention and keeps them exploring our content.


    Our "consistently inconsistent" design philosophy is a delicate balance between uniformity and unpredictability. By maintaining consistency in our layout and centering elements within a specified width, we establish a sense of harmony and cohesion. On the other hand, our varying fonts, button designs, and box designs inject a sense of unpredictability and individuality, preventing visual monotony and encouraging users to engage with different elements of our website.

    Colors

    SkoHubben does not focus on utilizing colors in the form of text, rather having the products and advertisements bring life to the website. While we still use modern and easy colors on buttons and fonts, we try to not over complicate things.

    • Colours should be used sparingly, have a functional purpose, and be accessible.
    • Rather than using shades of grey, use shades of navy to create a more cohesive aesthetic.
    • Associate buttons and actions with dark colors (green for actions relating to payment).

    Typography

    Typography is an essential aspect of the overall design of a website, as it ensures readability, consistency, and aesthetic appeal. This section outlines the usage guidelines for the fonts selected for our website. Adhering to these guidelines will maintain visual harmony and improve user experience. The fonts SkoHubben utilizes are as follows:

    • Varela Round (Secondary choice for general text)
    • Open Sans & Mulish (Header)
    • Nunito (Product boxes and main body text choice)
    • Poppins (Footer)
    • Akzidenz Grotesk BQ Medium (Buttons)
    • Roboto (Designmanual)

    Varela Round

    Varela Round should be used as the secondary choice for all general text on the website. This font provides a clean and friendly appearance that complements the overall design. Use Varela Round at a minimum size of 16px and a maximum of 18px.

    Mulish and Open Sans

    Mulish and Open Sans should be used for headers throughout the website. These fonts offer a balance between modern and classic design, providing a professional feel. For main headers (H1), use a font size of 36px. For subheaders (H2, H3), use font sizes of 24px and 18px, respectively. Adjust the font-weight as necessary to maintain legibility and contrast.

    Nunito

    Nunito should be used for product boxes and as the primary choice for main body text. This font offers a smooth and rounded look, ensuring easy readability. For body text, use a font size of 16px and adjust line-height to 1.5 for optimal readability. For product boxes, use a font size of 18px to emphasize product information.

    Poppins

    Poppins should be used for the footer section of the website. The font's geometric design adds a contemporary touch, contributing to the website's overall modern feel. Use a font size of 14px for footer text, and apply appropriate font-weight and line-height for readability and visual balance.

    Akzidenz Grotesk BQ Medium

    Akzidenz Grotesk BQ Medium should be used for all buttons on the website. This font provides a bold and functional look, ensuring that buttons are easily distinguishable and accessible. Use a font size of 16px for button text and adjust font-weight and letter-spacing as needed for clarity.

    Roboto

    Roboto should be used for the design manual itself. Its clean and neutral design ensures that the manual remains easy to read and navigate. Use font sizes of 18px for main headers, 16px for subheaders, and 14px for body text. Adjust line-height, font-weight, and letter-spacing as needed for optimal readability.

    Colors and contrast

    Maintain a high contrast between text and background colors to ensure readability. Use dark text on light backgrounds and vice versa.

    Black Version

    The black version of our logo is the primary and most commonly used logo. It should be used on white or light backgrounds to ensure optimal contrast and visibility. The black logo is ideal for use in main content areas, headers, and wherever the background is predominantly white or light-colored. Follow these guidelines when using the black logo:

    • Ensure a clear space around the logo equivalent to the height of the letter 'O' in the logo to maintain its visual impact and avoid clutter.
    • Do not alter the proportions, colors, or elements of the logo.
    • Avoid placing the black logo on dark backgrounds, as it will compromise visibility and contrast.

    White Version

    The white version of our logo serves as an alternative option for use on dark backgrounds, such as footers or sections with black or dark-colored backgrounds. The white logo provides a clean and modern look while maintaining readability and contrast. Follow these guidelines when using the white logo:

    • Similar to the black logo, ensure a clear space around the white logo equivalent to the height of the letter 'O' in the logo to maintain its visual impact and avoid clutter.
    • Do not alter the proportions, colors, or elements of the logo.
    • Avoid placing the white logo on light backgrounds, as it will compromise visibility and contrast.

    Right Usage

    • Always use the provided logo files to maintain quality and consistency.
    • Do not stretch, skew, or distort the logo in any way.
    • Use the appropriate logo version according to the background color to ensure optimal visibility and contrast.
    • Avoid placing the logo over busy or cluttered backgrounds, as it may hinder its readability and recognition.

      • By following these logo usage guidelines, we can maintain a consistent and recognizable brand identity across all platforms and media. The proper use of our logo enhances our brand's visual impact and contributes to the overall design coherence.
